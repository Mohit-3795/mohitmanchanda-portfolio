About

Hi, I’m Mohit. I work where strategy becomes execution.

I’m a Senior Operations & Sustainability Manager at THEY, a strategy and design consultancy in New Delhi. For the last five years I’ve worked across creative direction, operations, sustainability reporting, and the unglamorous middle layer of project management that actually decides whether the work ships.

My primary engagement is with Motherson Group — a ~$14bn global auto-components leader with 400+ plants across 44 countries, divisions spanning India, Germany, the UK, and Asia. A lot of the job is sitting with senior leaders, understanding what they actually need — often before it’s fully articulated — and then mobilising my teams and specialists to deliver it: listed-company annual reports, leadership presentations crafted with the Chairman’s office, sustainability reporting, and the production behind corporate films.

And when the gap is too wide to bridge with a strategy deck or a spreadsheet, I close it myself. I taught myself to build the application. Three targeted AI solutions now run in production at THEY — the B Corp dashboard, the slide library, and the story tracker for Motherson’s Vision 2030 initiative — proof, more than anything, that I’d rather build the fix than wait for one.

I studied Business Management at Vrije Universiteit Brussel in Belgium (2020–22), and before that completed my undergraduate at GGSIPU in New Delhi. The detour through Europe taught me that great operations is invisible — if the work feels effortless to the audience, someone backstage did the heavy lifting.
